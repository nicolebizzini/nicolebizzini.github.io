About

I am a biomedical engineer finishing my M.Sc. in Bioengineering (Neuroengineering & Neurotechnologies) at the University of Genoa, currently a Visiting Research Scholar at NYU Langone Health in the Neural Interface Engineering Lab.

My work focuses on machine learning for physiological and imaging data: real-time neuron segmentation from two-photon calcium imaging, biosignal processing (EMG, NIRS, electrophysiology), and wearable sensing. I enjoy building end-to-end pipelines that go from raw data acquisition to interpretable, actionable metrics.
